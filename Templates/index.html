About the two Disney parks, Land and Sea

Tokyo Disneyland

Tokyo Disneyland is a theme park located in Maihama made up of seven themed lands and features exciting thrill rides and parades. The lands include World Bazaar, Tomorrowland, Toontown, Fantasyland, Critter Country, Westernland, Adventureland. This park is modelled afer Disneyland in Anaheim and the Magic Kingdom in Orlando and is ideal for kids and kids-at-heart!

Tokyo Disneysea

Tokyo Disneysea is a theme park located in Maihama that is unique to Japan. Inspired by the myths and legends of the sea, Tokyo Disneysea is made up of seven themed ports of call centred around a volcano: Mediterranean Harbor, Mysterious Island, Mermaid Lagoon, Arabian Coast, Lost River Delta, Port Discovery and American Waterfront. This park is especially popular among young adults and those looking for a more refined, grown-up theme park experience.

Recommendations

The busiest day in Disneyland and Disneysea is Saturday, followed by Sunday, Monday and Friday.

We would advise visitors to plan their trip to fall on Tuesday, Wednesday and Thursday and include either Monday or Friday if you're planning to spend two days in each park.

The busiest months in Disneyland and Disneysea are January (school holidays and public holidays), May (Golden Week), August (school holidays), October (Halloween theming in the parks) and December (Christmas theming in the parks).

We would advise visitors to plan their trip to avoid these months as domestic tourism is at its highest. However, the Halloween and Christmas theming and seasonal food offerings are particularly renowned and well worth braving the crowds to enjoy.

The ideal months to visit are February, June, September, and November.
